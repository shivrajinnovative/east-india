East India Power Engineers (EIPE) was established in 2009 and EIPE works across the value chain from consulting, installation, execution to maintenance in the commercial, industrial, and retail sectors

The company was started by a group of young engineers with significant experience in handling MV-LV Switchgears & Transformers of all manufacturer’s up to 33KV.

EIPE having authorized service center for Schneider Electric India Pvt. Ltd. (MV & LV works), since 2009. In Mumbai & Goa region

In addition electrical engineering projects & after sales service support , Works in large organizations. EIPE assists its customers in reducing electricity consumption by designing, developing, manufacturing, owning and operating efficient, environmentally sound energy saving projects

The flexible nature of our resource structure and country-wide presence has allowed EIPE to build a brand and reputation for providing superior quality services in a cost and time-efficient manner.EIPE has forayed into the Turnkey Contracting space by providing an entire array of infrastructural and interior works.

EIPE is a multi-state licensed contractor and certified ISO 9001:2015

East india power Engineers is now owned by Mr. Ghanshaym M. Gawade of whom possess vast amounts of experience in the electrical, power and engineering industry in India.
